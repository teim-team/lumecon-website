Economic impact and policy analysis are strategic tools that pay for themselves

Economic impact and policy analysis measures how an organization's spending, hiring, and operations ripple through a regional economy — and how policy decisions shape who benefits. It turns budgets, programs, and policy alternatives into a clear map of consequences.

Traditional tools rely on modeled estimates and outdated national averages that are only updated every few years. Lumecon applies rigorous methods to administrative and high-frequency data — so your results reflect your economy, not a generic assumption.

Secure funding

Show funders, councils, and legislators exactly what your organization contributes. Impact numbers turn budget conversations into investment conversations.

Compare scenarios

What happens if you cut a program? Add 20 positions? Expand operations? Change a policy? Model the economic consequences of strategic decisions before you commit the resources.

Streamline compliance

Federal grants, foundation reporting, and regulatory reviews often require the same economic data. Generate compliance-ready outputs from analysis you're already running.

Tell your story

Annual reports, press releases, community presentations. Impact data makes the case in language stakeholders understand — people supported, dollars circulated.

Cedar™ is Lumecon's AI-powered assistant for economic and policy analysis. It works with messy, real-world data, clearly telling you what it needs and why, and walks you through each step so you can understand economic impact, test assumptions, and make informed strategic decisions. No consultant or overpriced software. Just you, your data, and a thought partner designed to support rigorous analysis without fabricating results. Cedar operates in secure, isolated environments, working only with the data you provide and preserving context across analyses.
