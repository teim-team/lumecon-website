Economic and policy analysis anyone can act on

Lumecon™ is an economic and policy analysis platform, offering specialized products for local, tribal, and global economic impact.

We deliver modern economic theory and novel modeling methods through affordable, web-based annual subscriptions so organizations of any size can access the caliber of analysis that once required expensive consultants. Built by social scientists, government professionals, and software engineers, Lumecon applies rigorous models to administrative and high-frequency data to make economies more visible and policy more actionable. Cedar

Your AI thought partner for economic and policy analysis.
We won't think for you.
We think with you.

Cedar™ is Lumecon's AI-powered assistant for economic and policy analysis. It works with messy, real-world data, clearly telling you what it needs and why, and walks you through each step so you can understand economic impact, test assumptions, and make informed strategic decisions. No consultant or overpriced software. Just you, your data, and a thought partner designed to support rigorous analysis without fabricating results. Cedar operates in secure, isolated environments, working only with the data you provide and preserving context across analyses.
